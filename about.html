Welcome to Empowering the Nation, a dedicated initiative founded by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Inspired by the lack of opportunities for her parents and elderly relatives to upskill or pursue formal education, Precious established this training school to support similarly affected members of her community.

Our programs are designed to enhance the employability of domestic workers and gardeners, enabling them to command higher wages and develop marketable skills. Through our training, participants gain the expertise to excel in their roles or even launch their own small businesses.
