First Aid

Six-Month Learnership R1,500

🎯 Purpose

To provide first aid awareness and basic life support skills that can save lives in emergency situations.

📚 Course Content

  • 🩹 Wounds and bleeding - Assessment and treatment of various wound types
  • 🔥 Burns and fractures - Emergency care for thermal injuries and bone fractures
  • 🚨 Emergency scene management - Safe approach and scene assessment
  • ❤️ Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) - Life-saving chest compressions and rescue breathing
  • 🫁 Respiratory distress - Managing choking, blocked airway, and breathing difficulties

⏱️ Course Duration

This comprehensive course runs for 12 weeks as part of our six-month learnership program.

🎓 What You'll Gain

Certification: Recognized first aid certificate upon completion
Employment Value: Highly sought-after skill by employers
Life Skills: Ability to help in emergency situations at home and work
