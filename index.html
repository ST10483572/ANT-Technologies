Welcome to Empowering the Nation

Empowering the Nation was established in 2022 by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. Her parents and other elderly relatives were never given the chance to upskill themselves or follow a formal educational qualification, so this training school is her way of supporting similarly affected members from her community.

🎯 Our Purpose

We upskill domestic workers and gardeners by making them more marketable when seeking employment and enabling them to be paid at a higher rate because of these additional skills. They can also be entrepreneurs and set up their own small business utilizing these newly obtained skills.

Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes!
